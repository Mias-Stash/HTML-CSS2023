What kind of photographs can I submit? Any other rules to keep in mind?

This page is made for educational purposes, as such, I will strictly forbid any submissions that go against school guidelines and policy. Please submit all images you intent to send in one email, this makes for less clutter and easier file organization on my end, making the submission process faster overall! I accept JPG, GIF, and PNG files for submission, all other file types will be ignored. Do not link this page on any external websites- the share buttons are purely for educational purposes. Use the chat room appropriately, I will not hesitate to close and remove it if I find the widget is being used for inappropriate reasons.

How do I submit a photograph?

Below this text is a form you will be able to fill in to create an email to me. Please include your name, Email, a description of the image(s), and attach the image(s) to your email. Doing this depends on the browser, or email application you are using. Typically. this is done by a small chain link icon towards the bottom of the “Compose message” box. When clicking on this, the user will be prompted to select or upload a file. Files can be dragged and dropped directly into the email composition box in many cases, as well.
